ADVANCED PARKING FEATURES

360 PARKING AID

Parking aid sensors on the rear, front, and sides provide real-time audible and visual feedback to indicate how close you are to obstacles.

PARK ASSIST

Your vehicle searches for and manoeuvres into both perpendicular and parallel parking spaces, while you control speed and direction.

360 SURROUND CAMERA

Provides a 360 exterior view via the Touchscreen, helping with various manoeuvres from parking by a kerb, to getting in and out of tight spaces and junctions.

CLEAR EXIT DETECTION

A warning light will flash on the rear doors to help everyone to exit the vehicle safely if there is oncoming traffic or other hazards.

EXPLORE DRIVING FEATURES

ADVANCED DRIVER ASSISTANCE FEATURES

REVERSE TRAFFIC DETECTION

The system warns you of vehicles, pedestrians or other hazards, approaching from either side of your vehicle. It will alert you with both audio and visual warnings so you know whats behind you even when the view is obstructed.

BLIND SPOT ASSIST

A flashing icon in the appropriate wing mirror will alert you to obstacles in, or quickly approaching, your blind spot.
